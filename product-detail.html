Description:

For those wishing to visit Pearl Harbor, but are staying on the Island of Kauai. This is the tour for you. This one-day inter-island tour from Kauai to Pearl Harbor includes round-trip airfare, admissions, and transportation on Oahu.

The flight from Kauai to Honolulu International Airport takes approximately 30 minutes and if you are sitting on the left side of the jet you can see Pearl Harbor as you enter.  When you arrive at Honolulu International Airport, a professional tour guide will greet begin your tour of Pearl Harbor. A few minutes later you will arrive at the Pearl Harbor Visitors’ Center of World War Two Valor in the Pacific National Park. The Arizona Memorial portion of the tour includes a 23 minute film about the 1941 Japanese attack, a Navy operated shuttle boat in the harbor, and reflection time on board the Arizona Memorial.

Adjacent to the Visitors’ center is the USS Bowfin Submarine. Admission is included and you will go below decks of this historic WWII submarine. It has been called the “Pearl Harbor Avenger”and was commissioned exactly one year after the attack on Pearl Harbor.

Next, our tours are authorized to drive across the Clarey Bridge to Ford Island. Ford Island is an active Navy base and has been used in films including In Harms Way with John Wayne and the movie Battleship.  Battleship was filmed on the the Battleship USS Missouri. This battleship has a much richer history.  She entered WWII in 1944 and fought in the battles of Iwo Jima and Okinawa. The USS Missouri is the site of the Japanese surrender in Tokyo Bay ending WWII. This tour includes a guided tour through the ship where you will marvel at the massive guns, look over the edge at the kamikaze dent, and stand on the site of the Japanese surrender.

The Pacific Aviation Museum is filled with WWII planes and stories about aviation at Pearl Harbor and the Pacific. New exhibits are always being added and the flight simulators are a blast.

After leaving Pearl Harbor, this tour drives through Honolulu’s historic district and Punchbowl National Memorial Cemetery of the Pacific.  This tour is your best choice if you want to make the most of your time on a tour from Kauai to Pearl Harbor. It is important to book early because this tour includes flights.
